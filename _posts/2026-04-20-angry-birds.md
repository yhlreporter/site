Those in between however, are angry.

## Emotional birds

Scientists have been wondering if birds feel emotions. And if they do, do they also experience emotional swings like humans do?
Perhaps even **hourly swings**?
Soaring with the wind under your wings look fun. Relaxing even. Surely birds are calm all the time, *no*?
