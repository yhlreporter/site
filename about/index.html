Past

I am a journalist from Malaysia who blends narrative writing with data analysis to examine sustainable use of natural resources. I graduated from University of California, Davis (Ph.D. Entomology) in 2010. I then co-founded Macaranga Media in 2019, Malaysia's only environmental newsroom. My stories have exposed and stopped illegal deforestation, and led to Orang Asli securing land in court against loggers. I have received international and national awards for data journalism, investigative reporting, and narrative writing. I am now a Nieman Fellow at Harvard University.
